🔧 **Installation**

To get your MULAA-MD bot up and running, follow these simple steps:

📌 **1. Fork and Star This Repository**
Show your support by forking 🍴 and starring ⭐ this repository!
[![Fork MULAA-MD](https://img.shields.io/badge/FORK-MULAA%20MD-BOTh?color=indigo&style=for-the-badge&logo=stackshare)](https://github.com/romeobwiii/MULAA-MD/fork) 📌 **2. Get Your Session ID**
A Session ID is essential for authenticating your bot with WhatsApp.
Click on the **Session ID button** below and follow the instructions. You will need to paste your WhatsApp number with the country code (e.g., `+23769xxxxxx`) to generate your unique ID.

[![Generate Session ID](https://img.shields.io/badge/GET%20SESSION%20ID-blueviolet?style=for-the-badge)](YOUR_SESSION_ID_GENERATOR_URL_HERE)
